About TSI

TPF Software has been a global leader in providing intuitive development tools for z/TPF and redefining business experiences.

TSI solves client challenges with an absolute balance of services in strategy, consulting, digital, technology, and operations. We partner with global businesses, driving innovation to improve how the world works and lives. TSI is proud to be a star in the Constellation Software (TSE:CSU) as a Juniper company, which is held by Vela Software.

Vision

We envision remaining true to our proud heritage and retaining our leadership position as one of the world’s principal providers of TPF tooling and services and prospering TSI as a brand that is associated with creating reliable and creative solutions, designed to improve the operational efficiency of airlines, airports and the banking systems around the world.

Mission

We are always on a mission to create reliable and secure software systems and domain-specific quality solutions to enhance the user experience and increase accessibility. In a post-modernization world, we strive to bring together our experience and expertise to provide enhanced value for businesses. We aim to maintain our excellency in helping the Aviation and Banking sector improve its operational efficiency through streamlined processes, automation, real-time data insight, and actionable intelligence by leveraging emerging Digital technologies.

History Timeline

Yesterday, and for the future

1986

A COMPANY WAS BORN

Information Technology company founded in 1986 pioneered the development of products based on IBM’s TPF and z/OS technologies that several fortune 500 companies use.

2000

A NEW MILLENNIUM

TSI plans to be more than a TPF product company. With the core being engineering, the company started to focus on long-term sustainability.

2005

FORAY INTO IT SERVICES

TSI entered the IT services segment, starting with legacy technologies and evolved into open systems and testing services.

2015

START OF DIGITAL ERA

TSI started working closely with airlines, helping them reduce their turnaround time by providing state-of-the-art ground handling technology through digital solutions.

2020

ACQUIRED BY CSI

Started a new era by becoming a part of Constellation Software Inc.

2022

FIRST ACQUISITION AS A CSI COMPANY

TSI along with Juniper group made its first acquisition with Airport Information Systems (AIS).
